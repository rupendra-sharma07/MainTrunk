This page is where you manage your tribute's administrators:

1. Current Administrators - This shows you all of your tribute's current administrators. You can delete users if you no longer want them to be an administrator. Note that the tribute creator can not be deleted as an administrator.

2. Add an Administrator - This is where you can add new administrators to your tribute. Enter the email address of the person you would like to include as an administrator for this tribute then click ""Add to administrators"". Administrators have access to add, delete, and edit all parts of a tribute.
