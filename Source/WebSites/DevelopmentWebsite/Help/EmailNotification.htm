The Message Notification section is where you control what emails Your Tribute sends you.

1. Tribute Notifications - You will receive tribute notifications whenever users add content to a tribute you have created, or the administrator of, or have marked as a favorite. In this section you can select what notifications you would like to receive. You can turn off notifications for specific tributes by unchecking the email notification box next to the tribute on the "My Tributes" page.

2. General Notifications - These are emails sent by Your Tribute. You can turn on/off being notified whenever you receive a message on Your Tribute. You can also select if you want to subscribe to our Newsletter.

NOTE: Even if you turn off all email notifications, we may sometimes need to email you important notices about your account (tribute expiry notices, etc).
