Members can only delete gifts they add. Tribute creators and administrators can delete all gifts added to their tribute.


To delete a gift:
1. You must be viewing the gift you would like to remove.
2. Log in to be able to delete the gift.
(Note that gifts that are given anonymously can only be deleted by the tribute creator and administrator.)
3. Click the "Delete" button next to the gift.
5. A dialog box will appear confirming the deletion of your gift.
