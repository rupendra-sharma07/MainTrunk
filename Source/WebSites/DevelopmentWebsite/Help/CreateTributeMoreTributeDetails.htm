1. Enter personal and biographical information on the person(s) you are creating the tribute for.

2. Use the default tribute photo or upload a custom photo:
a) Click the "Upload Photo" button.
b) Click "Browse..." and choose the photo you would like to upload and then click "Open".
c) Click "Upload Photo" to upload your photo.
d) Move the image cropper box over the photo to select the portion you would like saved. You can see how the photo will appear on the website in the preview window.
e) Click "Done" to finish uploading the photo.

3. Enter a custom welcome message or use the default text we have provided. The welcome message will appear on the tribute homepage, and will help to introduce people to your website.
