Photo albums are an excellent way to keep your photos organized. There is no limit to the number of photo albums you can create, but note that a photo album must contain at least 1 photo and is limited to a maximum of 60.


To create a new album and add photos to it:
1. Go to the tribute's photos page by clicking the "Photos" tab.
2. Log in to be able to create a photo album.
3. Select "Create an Album" from the "Tribute Tools".
4. You will be prompted to install an application that will make uploading your photos quick and easy. Please click "Trust" or "Allow" to any prompts that appear while the application is installing.
5. Provide an "Album Name" and optional "Album Description".
6. Select the photo(s) you would like to add to your photo album. Hold the "Shift" or "Ctrl" key to select multiple photos. Then click the "Add Photo" button.
7. Add an optional "Title" and "Description" to each of your photos.
8. Click "Create Album and Upload Photos" to create your photo album.
