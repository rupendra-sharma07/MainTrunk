The Profile Settings section is where you can update your personal information.

To change your profile picture:
1. Select "Choose Photo"
2. Click on the photo you would like to use
3. Click the "Save Changes" button

or, to upload a custom profile photo:
1. Click "Upload Photo"
2. Click "Browse..." and choose the photo you would like to upload and then click "Open".
3. Click "Upload Photo" to upload your photo.
4. Move the image cropper box over the photo to select the portion you would like saved. You can see how the photo will appear on the website in the preview window.
5. Click "Done" to finish uploading the photo.
6. Click the "Save Changes" button.

To change your profile information:
1. Edit the information you would like changed. Note that you cannot change your username.
2. Click the "Save Changes" button
