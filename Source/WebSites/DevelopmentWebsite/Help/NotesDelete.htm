Only the Tribute creators and administrators can delete a note.

To delete a note:

1. You must be viewing the note would like to remove.
2. Log in to be able to delete the note.
3. Select "Edit Note" from the "Tribute Tools"
4. Click the "Delete Note" button located at the bottom of the page.
5. A dialog box will appear confirming the deletion of your note. Note that when you delete a note all of the notes comments will also be deleted.
