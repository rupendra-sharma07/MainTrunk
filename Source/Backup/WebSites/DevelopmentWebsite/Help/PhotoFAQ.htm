How many photo albums can I create?
You are able to create as many photo albums as you like!


How many photos can I add?
You are able to add as many photos as you like! There is a maximum of 60 photos allowed per photo album.


How do I share a photo or photo album with people who do not use Your Tribute?
Your Tribute provides links that make sharing photos and photo albums quick and easy. View the photo or photo album you would like to share then select "Email this page" from the "Tribute Tools". Enter your friend(s) email address and they will be sent a link to view the photo or photo album.


What do I do if I see an offensive photo or comment?
1. If you feel a photo or comment that has been posted is offensive, please talk to the person who posted it. Click the users name to view their profile and send them a polite message. In most cases the user will be happy to remove the offensive material.

2. If the user does not remove the offensive photo or comment you can report it to the tribute creator or administrator. The tribute creator and administrators are listed on the tribute homepage under "About This Tribute". Click the users name to view their profile and send them a message.

3. If the photo or comment is still not removed you can contact the Your Tribute support team using the "Contact" link at the bottom of every page. Please be sure to included a link to the photo or comment and a detailed description so that we can easily located the post in question. Note that Your Tribute will ONLY remove photos and comments that violate our Terms of Use.
