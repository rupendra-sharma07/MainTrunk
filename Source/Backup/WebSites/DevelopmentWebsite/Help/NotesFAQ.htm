How many notes can I write?
There is no limit to the number of notes that you can write.

What is the maximum length of the note?
Notes can be up to a maximum of 10000 characters. You will receive a warning that you need to reduce the size of your note if you try to post a note that is too large.

How do I add a link in my note?
To have a link properly appear in your note you must include "http://" before the domain. For example, "http://www.yourtribute.com"

How do I share a note with people who do not use Your Tribute?
Your Tribute provides links that make sharing your note quick and easy. View the note you would like to share then select "Email this page" from the "Tribute Tools". Enter your friend(s) email address and they will be sent a link to view the note.

What do I do if I see an offensive note or comment?
1. If you feel a note or comment that has been posted is offensive, please talk to the person who posted it. Click the users name to view their profile and send them a polite message. In most cases the user will be happy to remove the offensive material.
2. If the user does not remove the offensive note or comment you can report it to the tribute creator or administrator. The tribute creator and administrators are listed on the tribute homepage under "About This Tribute". Click the users name to view their profile and send them a message.
3. If the note or comment is still not removed you can contact the Your Tribute support team using the "Contact" link at the bottom of every page. Please be sure to included a link to the note or comment and a detailed description so that we can easily located the post in question. Note that Your Tribute will ONLY remove notes and comments that violate our Terms of Use.
