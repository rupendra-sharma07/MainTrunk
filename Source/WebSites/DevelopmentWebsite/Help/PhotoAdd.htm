Photos can be added to an existing album by any registered user. Note that albums are limited to 60 photos and if the album is almost full you may want to create a new album.


To add photos to an existing album:
1. Go to the tribute's photos page by clicking the "Photos" tab.
2. Open a photo album by clicking on the photo album's image.
3. Log in to be able to add photos to the album.
4. Select "Add a Photo" from the "Tribute Tools".
5. You will be prompted to install an application that will make uploading your photos quick and easy. Please click "Trust" or "Allow" to any prompts that appear while the application is installing.
6. Select the photo(s) you would like to add to the album. Hold the "Shift" or "Ctrl" key to select multiple photos. Then click the "Add Photo" button.
7. Add an optional "Title" and "Description" to each of your photos.
8. Click "Save Album and Upload Photos" to add your photos to the album.
