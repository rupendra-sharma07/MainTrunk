Only the Tribute creators and administrators can change the tribute photo.

To change the tribute photo:

1. Go to the story page by clicking the "Story" tab.
2. Log in to be able to edit the story.
3. Click "Edit" next to the "Personal Detail" header.
4. Click the "Upload an Image" button.
5. Click "Browse..." and choose the photo you would like to upload and then click "Open".
6. Click "Upload Photo" to upload your photo.
7. Move the image cropper box over the photo to select the portion you would like saved. You can see how the photo will appear on the website in the preview window.
8. Click "Done" to finish uploading the photo.
9. Click "Save" to update the tribute photo.
