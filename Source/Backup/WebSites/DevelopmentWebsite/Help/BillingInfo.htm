The Billing Info section shows your billing history and credit card information.

1. Billing History - This shows you a history of all transactions on Your Tribute. Click to "View Receipt" to print a receipt from a previous transaction.

2. Credit Card Info - Add a credit card to make paying for tributes in the future faster and easier. You can also remove a credit card or modify it's information. Note that all billing information is securely stored on our servers.
