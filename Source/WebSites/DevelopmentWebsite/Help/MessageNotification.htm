1. Log in to Your Tribute.
2. Click the "My Profile" link in the header.
3. Click the "My Profile" tab.
4. Click the "Email Notifications" tab.
5. Under "General Notifications" you can turn on or off Your Tribute emailing you when you have a new message.

When Message Notification is on, Your Tribute will send an email, to the email address you used when creating your account, notifying you that a user has sent you a new message.
