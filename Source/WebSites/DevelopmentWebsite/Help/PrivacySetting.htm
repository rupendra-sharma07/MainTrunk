The Privacy Settings section is where you can modify what information other users can see.

1. Name Settings - Choose whether or not other users can see your full name or your username. Your name appears in your profile and on the website wherever you post content.

2. Location Settings - Choose whether or not other users can see your location. Your location appears in your profile and on the website wherever you post content.

3. Message Settings - Choose whether or not other users can send you messages. Note that users will never see your email address and they can only send you messages locally on Your Tribute.
