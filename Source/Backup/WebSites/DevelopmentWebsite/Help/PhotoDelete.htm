Members can only delete albums they create and photos they add. Tribute creators and administrators can delete all albums and photos added to their tribute.


To delete an album:
1. You must be viewing the photo album you would like to remove.
2. Log in to be able to delete the album.
3. Select "Edit Album" from the "Tribute Tools".
4. Click the "Delete Album" button located at the bottom of the page.
5. A dialog box will appear confirming the deletion of your album. Note that when you delete an album all of the photos and comments in the album will be permanently deleted.


To delete a photo:
1. You must be viewing the photo you would like to remove.
2. Log in to be able to delete the photo.
3. Select "Edit this photo" from the "Tribute Tools".
4. Click the "Delete Photo" button located at the bottom of the page.
5. A dialog box will appear confirming the deletion of your photo. Note that when you delete a photo all of the photo comments will also be removed.
