Only the Tribute creators and administrators can modify the "more about" section.

To add more about the person:

1. Go to the story page by clicking the "Story" tab.
2. Log in to be able to edit the story.
3. Click "Add a Topic" under the "More About" header.
4. Select a topic from the dropdown box or choose "Other Topic" to create your own.
5. Enter your text into the text box.
6. Click "Save" to record your new topic.
