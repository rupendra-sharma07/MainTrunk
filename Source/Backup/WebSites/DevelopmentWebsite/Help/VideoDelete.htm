Members can only delete videos they add. Tribute creators and administrators can delete all videos added to their tribute.


To delete a video:

1. You must be viewing the video you would like to remove.
2. Log in to be able to delete the video.
3. Select "Edit Video" from the "Tribute Tools".
4. Click the "Delete Video" button located at the bottom of the page.
5. A dialog box will appear confirming the deletion of your video. Note that when you delete a video all of the video comments will also be deleted.
