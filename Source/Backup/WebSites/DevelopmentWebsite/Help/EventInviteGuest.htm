To invite guests to a new event, you will be able to do this after entering your event details and clicking "Save and Invite Guests".

To invite guests to an existing event, from the event page click "Edit Event" and then click the "Invite Guests" button.

Inviting Guests:

1. To Import Contacts from an Email Service, click the email service logo then enter your username and password and click "Log in". A window will appear with your contacts. Select the contacts from the list you would like to invite and then click the "Add" button.
2. To manually add contacts, enter the email addresses into the box below "Enter Email Addresses". Separate emails with a ',' or a ';'. Click the "Add" button to add the contacts to your invitation list.
3. After adding your contacts to the "Guests to Invite" list, click the "Invite Guests" button.
4. A window will appear confirming an invitation has been sent to your guests.
