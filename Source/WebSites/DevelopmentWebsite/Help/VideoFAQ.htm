How many videos can I add?
You are able to add as many videos as you like.


How do I share a video with people who do not use Your Tribute?
Your Tribute provides links that make sharing videos quick and easy. View the video you would like to share then select "Email this page" from the "Tribute Tools". Enter your friend(s) email address and they will be sent a link to view the video.


What do I do if I see an offensive video or comment?

1. If you feel a video or comment that has been posted is offensive, please talk to the person who posted it. Click the users name to view their profile and send them a polite message. In most cases the user will be happy to remove the offensive material.

2. If the user does not remove the offensive video or comment you can report it to the tribute creator or administrator. The tribute creator and administrators are listed on the tribute homepage under "About This Tribute". Click the users name to view their profile and send them a message.

3. If the video or comment is still not removed you can contact the Your Tribute support team using the "Contact" link at the bottom of every page. Please be sure to included a link to the video or comment and a detailed description so that we can easily located the post in question. Note that Your Tribute will ONLY remove videos and comments that violate our Terms of Use.
