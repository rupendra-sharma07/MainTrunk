How many people can I invite to an event?
You can invite as many people to an event as you like.

can I invite to an event?
You can invite anyone who has a valid email address.

do I share an event with people who do not use Your Tribute?
Your Tribute provides links that make sharing your event quick and easy. View the event you would like to share then select "Email this page" from the "Tribute Tools". Enter your friend(s) email address and they will be sent a link to view the event. Note that you must use the RSVP feature if you would like to invite guests to your event.
