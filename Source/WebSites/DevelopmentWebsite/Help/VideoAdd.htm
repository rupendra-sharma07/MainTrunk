Video can be added by any registered user. Note that Your Tribute allows you to add videos that are uploaded to YouTube. You are not able to upload videos directly to Your Tribute.


For instructions on uploading videos to YouTube follow this link http://help.youtube.com/support/youtube/bin/topic.py?topic=10524


To add a video:

1. Upload your video to YouTube and save the Video URL or Video ID.
2. Go to the video page by clicking the "Videos" tab.
3. Log in to be able to add a video.
4. Select "Add a Video" from the "Tribute Tools".
5. Provide a "Video Name" and optional "Video Description".
6. Enter the YouTube Video URL or Video ID


What is my "Video ID"?
The Video ID is alphanumeric and comes after “watch?v=”. The Video ID is highlighted in BOLD in this sample URL: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a1BcD2ef3gh


What is my "Video URL"?
The Video URL must start with “http://www.” and end with the Video ID (i.e. “a1BcD2ef3gh”). This is a sample Video URL http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=a1BcD2ef3gh
