1. Select the level of privacy for your tribute:

a) Public - Public tributes can be found by searching for a tribute. This type of tribute could also be added to the Featured Tributes list when they are paid for.

b) Private - Private tributes will not show up in search results, and will not be included in the Featured Tributes list. These types of tributes will still be accessible to all users (that is they wont be password protected).


2. Add an Administrator - This is where you can add administrators to your tribute. Enter the email address of the person you would like to include as an administrator for the tribute. To add additional administrators click "Add another administrator". Administrators have access to add, delete, and edit all parts of a tribute.
