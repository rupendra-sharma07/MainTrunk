When you are invited to an event you will receive a notification email.

1. Click the link in the email to RSVP.
2. You will be taken to Your Tribute and will be required to log in or register if you are not a registered user.
3. After logging in you can decide whether you are "attending", "maybe attending" or "not attending" and click "RSVP"

To change your RSVP status:

1. You must be viewing the event page.
2. Log in to be able to change your RSVP status.
3. At the bottom of the page choose whether you are "attending", "maybe attending" or "not attending" and click "RSVP".
