How many gifts can my tribute have?
Users can give an unlimited number of gifts in your tribute.


How do I share the gifts with people who do not use Your Tribute?
Your Tribute provides links that make sharing gifts quick and easy. View the page with the gift you would like to share then select "Email this page" from the "Tribute Tools". Enter your friend(s) email address and they will be sent a link to view the gifts page.


What do I do if I see an offensive gift message?
1. If you feel a gift message that has been posted is offensive, please talk to the person who posted it. Click the users name to view their profile and send them a polite message. In most cases the user will be happy to remove the offensive material.

2. If the user does not remove the offensive gift message you can report it to the tribute creator or administrator. The tribute creator and administrators are listed on the tribute homepage under "About This Tribute". Click the users name to view their profile and send them a message.

3. If the gift message is still not removed you can contact the Your Tribute support team using the "Contact" link at the bottom of every page. Please be sure to included a link to gift message and a detailed description so that we can easily located the message in question. Note that Your Tribute will ONLY remove gift messages that violate our Terms of Use.
